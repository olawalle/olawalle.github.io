Vision Statement

Townsend Library seeks to be the “heart” of the academic and research culture of the campus, and to contribute to its intellectual activities. The Library envisions that it will fulfill its unique role as a major contributor to the academic, cultural, and social development of the university community

Mission Statement

Our Library seeks to enhance the scholarly pursuit of knowledge, to foster intellectual curiosity, and to promote life-long learning and research through our innovative services, resources, and cultural programs, which support the Prairie View A&M University’s global mission of teaching, service, and research.
